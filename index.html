Our Mission

The Midwest Rainbow Research Institute is dedicated to the production of credible publications revolving around the affairs and issues facing the LGBTQ+ community through field research, policy analysis, and community dialogue across the region.

Our Vision

We envision communities where LGBTQ+ individuals are free to express their identities without fear of violence, discrimination, or persecution through public education and policy research.
